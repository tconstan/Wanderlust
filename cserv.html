Jewelry Care
The following tips will help you keep your jewelry at its best condition:

  • -   Jewelry should be put on last and taken off first while dressing.
  • -   Store in a cool and dry place.
  • -   Avoid contact with chemicals such as hairspray or perfume.
  • -   Clean your piece with a soft cloth and mild detergent with water if necessary. Rinse with clean water and dry with a soft cloth.
