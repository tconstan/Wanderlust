ABOUT US
What does Wanderlust mean? Wanderlust is a strong desire for travel.

Wanderlust Accessories founder and owner Carolyn Chen has always been a free spirit who loves to explore and try new things. Throughout the years, she has been infatuated with the freedom of art and also the power of expression through fashion. At Wanderlust, the inspiration is derived from various experiences, places, ethnicities and cultures. With all the pieces being handmade, she aims to create high-quality pieces that are simple enough to be worn everyday and yet add something special to every outfit.
